Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zat?
Zat is an instant exchange all-in-one. Zat allows you to store the cryptocurrency of your choice and convert it to another as fast as possible.

How does Zat work?
You hold the private keys. We supply the exchanging. Zat automatically trades your cryptocurrency to another using an algorithm on various platforms without requiring you to hold crypto with us.

How much can I exchange and how does it work?
Depending on the pair, you can exchange up to $30,000 at once but we never limit how many times you can exchange. We will start sending your funds once the transaction reaches the minimum number of confirmations, normally within 10 to 30 minutes.
